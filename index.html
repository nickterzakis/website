About Me

Engineer.

I'm a Senior Software Engineer based in Pittsburgh with over 7 years of experience building scalable, mission-critical systems. At FedEx, I architect and own full-stack solutions that process over 1.2 million transactions daily — from Spring Boot microservices and native Android apps to optimized Oracle SQL and enterprise CI/CD pipelines.

I specialize in modernizing legacy systems, native Android development, distributed architectures, and building the observability infrastructure (Splunk, AppDynamics, DynaTrace) to keep everything running reliably at scale.

Away from the terminal, I create music under the name The Kid In The Red Hoodie — a blend of indie, lofi guitar loops, ambient textures, and alternative rock influences.
